Our Origin Story

A Legend Born in 2019

Founded by Mr. Rajveer "Raj" Singhania after watching exactly one episode of Hotel Hell. He decided, "How hard can it be?"

Today, we proudly serve guests from across the globe — mostly because they couldn't find the other hotel they actually booked. Our doors are now installed the right way. Mostly.
